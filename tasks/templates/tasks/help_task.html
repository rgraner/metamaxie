Let's take the following Target as an example. You can input up 5 tasks conditions as shown below:

If the average produced by the scholar is smaller than 75 SLP's, I will receive 60% of the amount at the claim.
Greater equal than 75 and smaller than 120 SLPâ€™s, I will receive 55%.
Greater equal than 120 and smaller than 135, I receive 50%.
Greater equal than 135 and smaller than 150, I receive 45%.
Greater equal than 150, I receive 40%.

Start by giving a name to the target to be identified when you assign it to a scholar. Then, input the smaller number in the following field, in this example 75. The next field, according to the example, is 60, which is the percentage you will receive if the scholar has produced less than 75 SLP's as an average. The following field labelled "otherwise, I receive.." means that if the scholar produced 75 or more SLP's you will input 55 according to the example and finally click the submit button.

After submitting the form, you will have 2 options:

1-Click on the "done" button and you will have a simple target described as follow:
"Average smaller than 75, I receive 60%. Greater equal than 75, I receive 55%."

2-Click on the "+" to continue inputting the others values presented in the example. From now, the process will be the same.

Fixed option

You must have noticed a fixed option by the third field. When selected, the value will be absolute, that is, Instead of 60% in the example, you can input 45. This will ensure to you a minimum amount of 45 SLP's for an average of less than 75 SLP's. This is applied only to this field.
